AWARNECESS PROGRAM ON CERICAL CANCER

The Interact Club of School utkarmit madhya vidayale organized an Awareness program on Cervical Cancer in the school premises. The senior girls from std X--XII and their mothers were present. It was an informative program. Girls interacted freely with the doctor. Dr. Amit Kumar n his team from Brahmananda Multi speciality Hospital conducted the session.

AWARNENCESS PROGRAM ON TRAFFIC RULES

Interactors of utkarmit maddhya vidayale.. Conducted an Awareness program on Traffic Rules at the Loyola.. Beldih Signal Crossing.... They stopped the People who were not wearing Helmets, Seat Belts or talking over mobile phones... Told them..the importance of following traffic Rules and put a SMILEY STICKER on their vehicles.... (Hundred Stickers) ... Which they think... will remind them of today's incident... Passers by appreciated the effort and thanked them
