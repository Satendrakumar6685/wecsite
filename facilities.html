FACILITIES

LAB

There are sevral labs physics lab, chemistry lab, computer lab, biology lab etc. The physics laboratory is equipped with the best and high quality equipment. All the instruments are accurate and are checked from time to time for any errors.
The chemistry lab is spacious, airy and well equipped with the latest apparatus to satisfy the inquisitive minds as they perform myriads of experiments. Safety precautions are taken by the chemistry faculty which is always available to students needing any help.

LIBRARY

The school provides a well-stocked library containing nearly 10,000 books, to set high standard of excellence in academics. Besides inculcating reading habit in them, it is a boon to the students for reference work and projects. Various magazines, news-papers and periodicals are provided to update students on current events. All library transactions are computerized.

PLAYGROUND

For the all-round development of students emphasis is also laid on co-curricular activities. Here we believe that a healthy mind resides in a healthy body.Physical training games and sports are compulsory activities on the school curriculum. The students are provided with suitable facilities to display their sporting skills.Several playgrounds and courts have been constructed within the premises of the school. Centrally located basket ball court attracts several aspiring sporting stars. There is a special ground for kho-kho etc.
